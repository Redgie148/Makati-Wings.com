About Us

Welcome to Makati Wings!




We are a family-owned restaurant located in the heart of Makati City, Philippines. We specialize in serving the best chicken wings in town, along with a variety of delicious sides and drinks.



At Makati Wings, we're committed to using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients in our dishes. Our menu features a variety of wing flavors, from classic buffalo to sweet and savory Asian-inspired sauces. We also offer a range of sides, including fries, onion rings, and salads, as well as a selection of beers and cocktails to complement your meal.



Thank you for choosing Makati Wings. We look forward to serving you and becoming your go-to spot for delicious wings and great times!
