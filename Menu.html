Menu

Welcome to our menu page! We offer a wide variety of delicious and satisfying dishes for any craving you may have. From our unlimited chicken wings with various flavors under the "Wings" category, to our delectable "Silog" Filipino breakfast options such as Tapa, Spam, Bangus, and Tocino, we've got you covered for any meal of the day. Our "Snacks" category includes Jalapeno Poppers, Burgers, Fries, Nachos, Kimchi, and Wing Dips that are perfect for sharing with friends or munching on as a quick bite. For drinks, we offer various kinds of coffee, juice, milk tea, and iced tea that you can enjoy anytime. Come and indulge in our scrumptious dishes and beverages!
